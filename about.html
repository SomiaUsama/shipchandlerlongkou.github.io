About Ship Chandler Longkou Co., Ltd.

Your Trusted Partner in Marine Supplies

Ship Chandler Longkou Co., Ltd. has been a leading provider of marine supplies and services, offering comprehensive solutions to shipping companies worldwide. We are committed to providing quality products and excellent service to ensure the smooth operations of vessels.

With decades of experience, our team offers a range of essential ship supplies including deck, engine, safety, and cabin stores, along with other technical equipment necessary for maritime operations. Our extensive inventory and tailored services ensure that every client's needs are met with the highest standards of quality and efficiency.
